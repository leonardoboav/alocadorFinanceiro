## What is asset allocation?

Dividing your wealth across asset classes (fixed income, stocks, real estate funds, international ETFs, etc.) according to a target percentage. The goal is to keep risk aligned with your plan over time, not to predict the market.

## How do we calculate deviations?

We compare each class's current percentage against your target. The difference in percentage points shows whether a class is above or below target. A negative deviation means you should direct new contributions there.

## How does the contribution suggestion work?

We calculate how much each class needs (in your portfolio's base currency) to reach its target after the new contribution is added. The contribution amount is distributed to the most underweight classes first. In v1, we never suggest selling — only where to buy.

## Why no automatic quotes?

We do not pull live prices in v1. You enter current values manually. This keeps the tool private, offline-capable, and simple. Automatic quotes are planned for a future version.

## Limits of this tool

- Does not consider taxes or brokerage fees
- Does not recommend specific securities
- Does not apply FX conversion automatically in v1 (user enters values in base currency)
- Is not a substitute for a licensed financial advisor
